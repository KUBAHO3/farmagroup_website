Elevating Business Performance Through Innovation

Founded in 2023, Framagroup provides technology consulting, design, and management services that align technology with business goals. Our expertise spans structured network design, network security, IP surveillance, IP telephony (VoIP), access control, intrusion detection, and smart automation systems.

Framagroup Logistic offers integrated logistics solutions that add value to your supply chain with long-term trust-based relationships.

Frequently Asked Questions

Explore answers to the most common questions about our services, implementation processes, and support offerings.

What industries do you serve with your technology and logistics solutions?

We work with businesses across sectors such as retail, education, manufacturing, real estate, logistics, and healthcare. Our flexible and scalable solutions are tailored to the needs of both small and large enterprises.

How long does it take to implement a structured cabling or IP surveillance system?

Project timelines vary based on the scope and environment. Typically, structured cabling or surveillance systems are implemented within 1 to 3 weeks, starting with a site assessment and followed by detailed planning and execution.

Do you provide support after installation?

Yes. We offer comprehensive post-installation support, including remote troubleshooting, maintenance plans, and system upgrades to ensure your infrastructure remains secure and effective.

Can your solutions integrate with existing systems?

Absolutely. Our systems are designed for seamless integration with existing IT infrastructure, whether it's enhancing your current surveillance system, upgrading your telephone network, or linking access control with HR systems.

Do you develop custom software?

Yes, we offer full-cycle custom software development. From mobile apps to enterprise systems, we design and build software tailored to your specific business needs, with full support for integration and scalability.

What’s the best way to get a quote for your services?

You can request a quote through our website's contact form or by emailing info@framagroup.co.mz. Please include project details or requirements so we can provide an accurate proposal.
